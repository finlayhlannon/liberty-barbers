ABOUT US

Liberty Barbers is a traditional barbershop in Victoria, BC, offering a range of grooming services for men. With a focus on classic barbering techniques, we provide haircuts, shaves, and beard trims in a relaxed and welcoming atmosphere.

Our skilled barbers are dedicated to providing quality service and creating a comfortable experience for every customer. Whether you're looking for a fresh haircut or a traditional hot towel shave, Liberty Barbers is committed to helping you look and feel your best.
